Explore Brooklyn

Welcome to Brooklyn. The South Eastern borough of NYC. It is a cultural icon with a distinct hip hop urban vibe that blends history with modern trends. There is diversity with a variety of cultures in foods and festivals from an immigrant history. Known landmarks such as the Brooklyn Bridge, Coney Island and more.

Attractions: Must See Spots In Brooklyn

View through the must see spots that you don't want to miss out on.

New York Transit Museum

The New York Transit Museum is the City's Public Transportation Museum that stores and displays historical artifacts of trains, buses, and stories of the transportation system. Location: 99 Schermerhorn St, Brooklyn, NY 11201.

Brooklyn Bridge

The Brooklyn Bridge is a cable-stayed suspension bridge that connects Mnahattan and Brooklyn across the east river. It showcases a stunning views for great photoshoots. And the experience going through is a great way of exercise. Location: New York, NY 10038.

Prospect Park

Prospect Park is a urban oasis park designed to cover woodlands, a lake, and meadows that support various spots such as a zoo, botanical gardens, museum, and events and films as a breakaway from the urban city. Location: Brooklyn, NY.

Coney Island

Coney Island is a neighborhood and entertainment area that offers the luna amusment park with rides. The beachfront to rest and boardwalk filled with sideline activites. And NYC's only aquarium. Location: Brooklyn, NY 11224.

Barclays Center

The Barclays Center is a multi-purpose indoor arena that is home to the brooklyn nets of the NBA. It also hosts premier concerts, boxing and other sporting events. Location: 620 Atlantic Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11217.
