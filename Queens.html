Explore Queens

Welcome to Queens. The eastern and largest borough of NYC. It is a diverse icon known for its world cuisine. There is also a strong local identity within the neighborhoods. Known landmarks include Citi field, Aquedact Racetrack and more.

Attractions: Must See Spots In Queens

View through the must see spots that you don't want to miss out on.

New York Hall Of Science

The New York Hall Of Science is a science museum that showcases more than 450 exhibits that explores physics, biology, and chemistry. Location: 47-01 111th St, Corona, NY 11368.

Flushing Meadows Corona Park

Flushing Meadows Corona Park is the 4th largest public park in NYC. It features green spaces for scenic sports and activites. It also features the Queens Zoo, Queens Museum, New York Hall of Science and more. Location: P5R5 + 2P, Queens, NY 11354.

Queens Center Mall

Queens Center Mall is a enclosed shopping mall that houses premier retail stores along with food dining courts. Location: 90-15 Queens Blvd, Elmhurst, NY 11373.

Astoria Park

Astoria Park is a public park that offers a beautiful pool, tennis courts, a track, trails and sport courts to take some time off. Location: 19 19th St, Astoria, NY 11105.

Museum Of The Moving Image

The Museum of The Moving Image is a media museum tht showcases aspects of movies, sound, lighting, animation and more experiences. Location: 36-01 35th Ave, Queens, NY 11106.
