Staten Island Page

Explore Staten Island

Welcome to Staten Island. The Southernmost borough of NYC. It is a historic icon known for its rich colonial history. It offers a quiter and more suburban vibe. Known landmarks include the historic richmond town, staten island ferry and more.

Attractions: Must See Spots In Staten Island

View through the must see spots that you don't want to miss out on.

Staten Island Children's Museum

The Staten Island Children's Museum is a children's museum that showcases a hands on museum with interactive exhibits and activites for children and families. Location: 1000 Richmond Terrace Building M Enter via the Fillmore St. Gate, Tysen St, Staten Island, NY 10301.

St. George Theater

The St. George Theater is a performing arts venue that hosts a variety of live performances and communitry driven art events. Location: 35 Hyatt St, Staten Island, NY 10301.

National Lighthouse Museum

The National Lighthouse Museum is a lighthouse museum that is dedicated to the history of lighthouses and their keepers in NYC. Location: 200 The, Promenade at Lighthouse Point, Staten Island, NY 10301.

Historic Richmond town

Historic Richmond town is a joint project that preserves the living history village including the hsitorical museum and arboretum. Location: 441 Clarke Ave, Staten Island, NY 10306.

Fort Wadsworth

Fort Wadsworth is a former United States military installation. It showcases the history of being a natural point for defense of the upper bay, Manhattan and beyond. Location: 150 S Weed Rd, Staten Island, NY 10305.
